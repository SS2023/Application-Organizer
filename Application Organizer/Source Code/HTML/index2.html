Welcome to Application Organizer!

We help people who are applying for internships or jobs track their applications and progress. Staying organized during the application process is essential.
This website can be used as a structuring tool to keep track of your progress.

Completed Applications

Keep track of your completed applications by going here. Simply hit the "Add New Application" button to add new applications. And also, keep all your applications organized by moving the applications to either offers or rejections, respectively. This section provides people with a portal to manage all their potential offers and make clearer plans around them.

Saved Applications

Keep track of your saved applications by going here. Simply hit the "Save a New Application" button to save new applications. And also, keep all your applications organized by priority. This section provides people with a portal to manage all their potential offers and make clearer plans around them.

Upcoming Interviews

Keep track of your Upcoming interviews by going here. Simply hit the "Add Interview" button to add upcoming interviews. This is a great way to keep all your upcoming interviews in check. This section provides people with a portal to manage all their potential offers and make clearer plans around them.

Offers

Got an Offer? Keep track of your offers by going here. Simply hit the "Add New Offer" button to add new offers. This is a great way to keep all your offers in check. You can also organize your offers from completed applications tab. This section provides people with a portal to manage all their potential offers and make clearer plans around them.
